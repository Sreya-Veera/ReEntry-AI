The global mobility industry is worth **$17 billion**. Thousands of tools exist to help people **LEAVE** their home countries - visa assistance, relocation services, immigration lawyers.

But what about the **120 million people** who **RETURN home** every decade?

- 73% of returnees report major difficulties
- Average return costs: $15K-30K in direct expenses

- Severe reverse culture shock (often worse than initial culture shock)
- **Zero existing solutions** for this journey

**People return "blind" - with hope, but no roadmap.**

## ğŸ’¡ Our Solution

ReEntry AI is the **first AI-powered platform** specifically designed for professionals planning their return home.

### ğŸ¤– Four Specialized AI Agents:

1. **Career Transition Agent**
   - Translates international roles to local market equivalents
   - Analyzes demand levels and salary ranges
   - Identifies skill gaps with actionable solutions
   - Forecasts hiring seasons and market trends

2. **Financial Modeling Agent**
   - Calculates relocation and setup costs
   - Projects income with purchasing power adjustments
   - Analyzes tax implications (exit & return)
   - Determines financial readiness with buffer recommendations

3. **Cultural Reintegration Agent**
   - Assesses reverse culture shock risk levels
   - Predicts adjustment timeline with peak difficulty periods
   - Provides evidence-based coping strategies
   - Offers psychological pattern analysis from returnee data

4. **Timing Optimization Agent**
   - Calculates optimal return date based on multiple factors
   - Weighs hiring cycles, financial readiness, and personal urgency
   - Provides alternative scenarios with trade-off analysis
   - Monitors key decision factors
